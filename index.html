About us

Crystal Green Nigerian Food Export is created to help our fellow Nigerians in diaspora to get access to native food items with ease and affordability. We believe that with the growing populations of Nigerians in diaspora there is a big demand for Nigerian food items and we are poised to serve you. We aim to bring Nigeria to you anywhere in the world. Our food items are affordable, of good quality and we try our best to offer exemplary services. Some of these food items, we source directly from farmers. Cost of shipment of food items differs from country to country. We are building a hub for Nigeria food items globally, Join us.
